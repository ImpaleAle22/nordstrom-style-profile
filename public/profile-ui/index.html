Privacy & Controls

Your data & privacy controls

Your Style Profile uses data from your Nordstrom account to personalize your shopping experience. We use this data to:
  • Recommend products that match your aesthetic
  • Curate collections tailored to your style
  • Provide relevant styling advice and content
  • Improve product discovery and search results
We DO NOT:
- Share your style data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising outside Nordstrom
- Sell or monetize your personal information
Reset Your Style Profile
If you'd like to start fresh, you can reset your Style Profile. This will clear all learned preferences and you'll need to retake the Style Quiz.
Download Your Data
You can download a complete export of your Style Profile data at any time.
